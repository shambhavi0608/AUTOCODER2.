A New Home, A Strange Presence

Two weeks after moving into my new apartment, I noticed something unsettling—a locked room that was always wet. Every morning, I’d find puddles, and by the window, a silent white pigeon watched me.

The Calls Begin

At 3:21 AM, I started receiving 21 missed calls from an unknown number. One night, I answered. Static. Then—“Help me.” The call cut off. The locked room’s door creaked open, revealing walls dripping with water. In the reflection, I saw her—a woman who wasn’t there.

The Truth Resurfaces

I discovered a woman named Aanya had drowned in that room two years ago. Her body was never found. Only her phone, still ringing, with 21 unanswered calls to her brother.

The Final Call

That night, the water rose. It reached my waist. The walls pulsed. Then—a hand burst from the water, dragging me under. My scream was swallowed by silence.

The Aftermath

By morning, my phone floated in the flood. One new notification: "21 missed calls from Unknown Number." And by the window, the white pigeon remained. Watching.
